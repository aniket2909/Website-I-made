In the kalyug, now people have started understanding the importance of kul devta. We are proud to be a member of "Bhambhi Kul". Now, the "Bhambhi Kul" clan has expanded and is spread all over the world. The samadhi sthal of our kul devta Sadguru Baba Molan Das Ji is located at Gagomahal, Tehsil Ajnala, District Amritsar, where a mela is organised every year to pay reverence to the elders and take the blessings of Baba Ji. People from all over India and even foriegn countries and neighbouring villages congregate in large number. The day of mela is on the first Sunday of Magghar month(desi calender). As per the rituals and traditions, on the day of mela eagles mark their presence by hovering over Samadhi Sthal. Prasad is offered to the eagles. After that the community lunch starts.
